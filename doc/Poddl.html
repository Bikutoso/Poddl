Overview

Poddl is a module for downloading Japanese audio clips.

Basic example

require "poddl"

words = [Poddl::Word.new("えき", "駅"),
         Poddl::Word.new("ピアノ")]

word.each do |word|
  word.download("/tmp") unless word.empty?
end
